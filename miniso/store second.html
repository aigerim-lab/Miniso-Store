About Us

MINISO

Is a lifestyle product retailer, offering high-quality household goods, cosmetics, food, and toys at affordable prices.

Following 7 years of rapid growth, MINISO has now opened more than 4,200 stores over 80 countries and regions, including the US, UK, Canada, Australia, Spain, UAE, India, and Mexico.




ESG Governance

MINISO attaches great importance to sustainable development that social and governance considerations into daily business operations and management.

It is committed to establishing a governance structure with clear responsibilities which improves the ESG management functions in combination with business development.




Environment

MINISO adheres to the philosophy of simple, natural and quality life while developing products.

We are committed to creating a better life with sustainable development by responding to climate change, creating low-carbon products, and practicing green operations.




Society

MINISO actively participates in public welfare and charity, assumes the responsibilities of a corporate citizen, fully respects and protects rights and interests of employees, provides healthy and safe working environment.




Governance

MINISO have established and continuously improved the internal governance mechanism, always guarding against potential risk points, and strongly supporting the Group's anti-corruption education.

Advocacy construction to improve our overall governance level and lay the foundation for the sustainable development of the Group.
